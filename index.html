01 about


Hi there, I'm Courtney and I'm so glad that you're here! As a current graduate student at the University of Denver, I am on my way to earning a Master degree in Library and Information Sciences. In my free time I love to read, explore and hike around Colorado, and plan for my next international travels.


In 2017 I embarked on my first solo travel experience to Belize and Guatemala. After 10 days abroad, I decided it was time for next big adventure. My ambitious intentions led me to booking a flight to Berlin, Germany to move abroad for the summer. For two and a half months I lived in an apartment in a suburb of Berlin called Lichtenberg. While living abroad, my love for traveling and exploring new cultures increased and that led me to planning for my trip back to Europe. After leaving Berlin and continuing my undergrad degree for the next couple of months, next thing I knew I was boarding my next flight to explore London and Dublin.


During my last year of undergrad in 2018, I was able to study abroad twice on two shortened trips. My first study abroad experience was to Germany. We started in Munich and from there went on to explore Nuremberg, Berchtesgaden, and Berlin. Shortly after my first study abroad adventure, I took a short 10 day trip to explore Barcelona, Madrid, and Lisbon. On my second study abroad experience, we took flight to Krakow, Poland. While there, we visted major historical sites and spent three days in Warsaw.


After crossing off several new countries on my travel bucket list, I later was selected for a writing internship in Big Island, Hawaii. Exploring Hawaii and living for a week in a condo with an oceanview was a dream. After the internship came to an end, I was so happy to see my name and writings appear in two books that we, as an internship group, published.
